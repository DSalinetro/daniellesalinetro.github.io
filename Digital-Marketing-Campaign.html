Creative Wellness Week

Digital Marketing Campaign — supporting creative professionals through mental health awareness with data-driven messaging and empathy-centered design.

Campaign Goal

Raise awareness about mental health challenges in creative industries while promoting wellness resources and community support for designers, artists, and creative professionals.

Target Audience

Creative professionals aged 25–45 experiencing work-related stress, burnout, or isolation. Primary focus on freelancers, agency workers, and independent artists.

Key Message

Your creativity and mental wellness are interconnected. Professional support designed specifically for creative minds helps you thrive personally and professionally.

Campaign Strategy

Data-driven content that validates creative professionals’ experiences while providing actionable resources, community connection, and professional mental health support.

Social Media Campaign
