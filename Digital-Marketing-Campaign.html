Creative Wellness Week

Digital Marketing Campaign - Supporting creative professionals through mental health awareness with data-driven messaging and empathy-centered design approach.

Campaign Goal

Raise awareness about mental health challenges in creative industries while promoting wellness resources and community support for designers, artists, and creative professionals.

Target Audience

Creative professionals aged 25-45 experiencing work-related stress, burnout, or isolation. Primary focus on freelancers, agency workers, and independent artists.

Key Message

Your creativity and mental wellness are interconnected. Professional support designed specifically for creative minds can help you thrive both personally and professionally.

Campaign Strategy

Data-driven content that validates creative professionals' experiences while providing actionable resources, community connection, and professional mental health support.

Social Media Campaign

Instagram and LinkedIn posts designed to engage creative professionals with relatable content and valuable insights

Campaign Strategy & Impact

Data-Driven Messaging

Using insights from our research to create messages that resonate with real experiences of creative professionals, building trust through validation and understanding.

Multi-Platform Approach

Coordinated campaign across social media, email, and web advertising ensures consistent messaging while adapting content format to each platform's unique audience behavior.

Empathy-Centered Design

Visual design reflects the warmth and professionalism of the Hearts & Minds brand, creating emotional connection while maintaining credibility and trust.

Community Building

Campaign focuses on reducing isolation by highlighting shared experiences and connecting creative professionals with resources and each other.

Actionable Resources

Every piece of content includes clear next steps, from downloadable guides to community connections, ensuring audience engagement translates to meaningful support.

Professional Development

Campaign balances mental health awareness with career growth messaging, showing how wellness and professional success are interconnected for creative professionals.
